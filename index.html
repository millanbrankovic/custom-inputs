Custom Checkbox & Radio Inputs

Default checkboxes and radio buttons are quite ugly and aren't friendly customizable - at least it was the case in the past. Luckily CSS3 offers a good solution - :checked pseudo class.

Also there are many icon fonts that can be used to avoid sprites importing and to reduce the number of HTTP requests. Editing sprites in Photoshop may be too annoying sometimes. Combination of icon fonts or/and ::before & ::after pseudo selectors is a much better and faster approach. And of course - it's retina optimized!
